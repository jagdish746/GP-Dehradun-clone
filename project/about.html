About Government Polytechnic Dehradun

The Government Polytechnic Dehradun (GPD) was established in 1980. GPD is affiliated to Uttarakhand board of technical education roorkee (UBTER) and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), Government of India . Government Polytechnic Dehradun offers quality technical education to the students from Uttarakhand.

Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education Roorkee has been setup by an act no – 27 of 2003 as passed by Uttarakhand legislative assembly and assented by Honorable Governor on 13-Janauary 2004 and notified on 15 – January 2004.
It provides for the establishment and constitution of Board of Technical Education in Uttarakhand and for matters connected there with or incidental there to.

LEADING TECHNICAL

Government Polytechnic Dehradun is a leading polytechnic institute offering Diploma programmes in several streams of Engineering and Pharmacy.

A campus with over 900 students, taught by highly distinguished faculty using the latest teaching aids, excellent hostel facilities, and a vision that drives us to ensure imparting wholesome technical education to our students.

An educational oasis located in the pristine Doon valley, the 12.85 acre GPD campus provides unparalled opportunity for students. A wealth of resources and support services help students get the most out of their time at GPD. From first rate libraries to innovative computer technology, students have access to many tools to explore academic interests. The campus offers diverse resources for the mind, body, and spirit and enough flexibility for students to explore them in their own distinctive ways

The GPD campus is just a short ride on public transport from the heart of downtown Dehradun and just 1.5 km from ISBT. No matter what your choice of study, the GPD campus will inspire you. As student at GPD, you’ll have all this and more at your doorstep.
